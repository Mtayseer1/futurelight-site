About Odoo Accounting

Odoo Accounting is a powerful, integrated financial management solution that helps businesses automate bookkeeping, manage invoices, and maintain accurate financial records in real time. At Future Lights Technologies, we implement and customize Odoo Accounting to meet the specific needs of organizations across Jordan and the GCC region.

Whether you operate a single branch or multiple subsidiaries, Odoo’s accounting suite supports multi-company consolidation, automated tax reporting, and bank synchronization. Our experts ensure your system complies with local financial regulations and VAT requirements while maintaining performance and scalability.

Key Features of Odoo Accounting

Invoice & Billing

Automate invoicing, recurring bills, and customer payments with digital receipts and smart reminders.

Bank Synchronization

Connect directly to your bank account for live balance updates and reconciliation automation.

Real-Time Financial Reports

Access real-time dashboards for profit & loss, balance sheet, aged receivables, and VAT summaries.

Multi-Currency & Tax Support

Manage international transactions, GCC VAT, and regional compliance with ease.

Automated Reconciliation

Save time with smart reconciliation suggestions and configurable matching rules.

Expense Management

Digitize expense tracking and integrate approvals directly into the accounting workflow.
