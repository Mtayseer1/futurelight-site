About Odoo Marketing Automation

The Odoo Marketing Automation module enables businesses to design automated workflows, send personalized emails, and track customer engagement. It empowers your team to nurture leads and retain customers effectively — all within one unified Odoo ecosystem.

At Future Lights Technologies, we help companies across Jordan and the GCC set up and optimize Odoo Marketing Automation for measurable growth and smarter communication.

Key Features

Email Campaigns

Create and schedule automated email campaigns tailored to customer behavior and preferences.

Automation Workflows

Analytics & Reporting

Measure open rates, click-throughs, and conversions with real-time dashboards and KPIs.
