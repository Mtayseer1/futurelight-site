Café Management System

An all-in-one solution for cafés and restaurants

Overview

Our Café Management System empowers cafés with seamless operations: from table reservations to kitchen order tracking and financial reporting.

Key Features

  • Table Management with real-time status updates
  • Waiter mobile app with notifications
  • QR code ordering for customers
  • Integrated payments & receipt printing
  • Kitchen display with live order updates
  • Menu Management (pricing, specials, availability)
  • Sales, expenses, and purchases reporting
  • Shift & cash register monitoring
  • Unpaid/complementary items tracking
  • Multi-café support (multi-tenancy)
