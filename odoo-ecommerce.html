About Odoo eCommerce

The Odoo eCommerce module enables businesses to create responsive, dynamic, and fully integrated online stores. It combines website design, product management, and order processing within a single Odoo ERP environment.

Future Lights Technologies delivers full Odoo eCommerce solutions — from setup and theme design to integration with accounting, CRM, and inventory — empowering Jordanian and GCC companies to sell smarter and faster.
