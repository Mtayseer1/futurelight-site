About Odoo Fleet Management

Odoo Fleet helps organizations manage vehicles, drivers, fuel logs, contracts, and maintenance schedules in one place. It simplifies fleet operations, reduces manual work, and provides accurate cost tracking for every vehicle.

Future Lights Technologies implements Odoo Fleet for logistics, transportation, construction, and corporate fleets — optimizing asset utilization and improving operational efficiency across Jordan and the GCC.

Key Odoo Fleet Features

Vehicle Tracking

Maintain complete records for every vehicle — model, license, acquisition date, and cost.

Fuel Management

Track fuel logs, costs, and efficiency metrics per vehicle and per driver.

Maintenance Scheduling

Plan preventive maintenance and manage repair costs with automatic reminders and records.

Integrated with Other Odoo Modules

Accounting

Automatically link vehicle expenses to journals for accurate reporting and budgeting.

HR

Assign vehicles to employees and track driver information with integrated HR records.

Purchase

Manage supplier contracts and vehicle-related purchase orders seamlessly.

Reporting

Monitor fleet performance, cost per kilometer, and efficiency trends through custom dashboards.
