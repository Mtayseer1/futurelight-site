About Odoo Sales

The Odoo Sales module streamlines your entire sales process within a unified ERP environment. From creating professional quotations and managing customer contracts to integrating with inventory and accounting, Odoo Sales enables businesses to accelerate deal cycles, improve accuracy, and enhance customer satisfaction. Whether you're a small business in Amman or an enterprise operating across the GCC, Future Lights Technologies ensures your sales workflows are optimized for success.

Core Features of Odoo Sales

Quotation & Order Management

Create, send, and convert quotations into confirmed sales orders. Manage customer pricing, discounts, and automated renewals effortlessly.

Automated Sales Workflow

Configure e-signatures, manage approvals, and connect your sales process directly to invoicing and delivery for a true quote-to-cash experience.

Performance Analytics

Get real-time sales insights, monitor KPIs, and forecast revenue using Odoo’s powerful reporting and dashboard features.

Integrated Sales Ecosystem

CRM

Convert leads into quotations directly from Odoo CRM for a seamless pipeline-to-order flow.

Accounting

Automatically generate invoices and track payments without manual data entry.

Inventory

Synchronize product availability, delivery orders, and stock updates in real time.

Marketing

Integrate email campaigns and automate follow-ups to boost conversions.
