About Odoo Human Resources

Odoo HR is a comprehensive Human Resources suite designed to simplify every stage of the employee journey — from recruitment and onboarding to payroll and performance evaluation. With Future Lights Technologies as your Odoo partner, you can automate HR processes, improve efficiency, and ensure compliance across your organization in Jordan and the GCC.

Whether you manage a small business or a large enterprise, Odoo HR gives you the tools to centralize employee data, monitor attendance, handle leaves, and streamline payroll processing in one integrated system.

Core Features of Odoo HR

Employee Management

Centralized database for employee information, contracts, and documents with secure access control.

Attendance & Leaves

Track employee attendance and leave requests with approval workflows and automated balances.

Payroll Management

Generate payslips, manage salary structures, and calculate benefits while complying with local labor laws.

Recruitment

Manage job postings, applications, interviews, and candidate evaluations in a streamlined workflow.

Performance & Appraisals

Conduct performance reviews and link results directly to employee development plans and goals.

Timesheets & Projects

Record work hours, link timesheets to projects, and generate productivity analytics for each department.

Integrated HR Ecosystem

Odoo HR seamlessly integrates with Payroll, Accounting, and Projects modules to unify your workforce and financial operations. Future Lights Technologies ensures full automation from employee time tracking to salary payments and expense accounting.

Our localized implementation aligns with Jordanian labor regulations and GCC HR compliance standards, offering bilingual interfaces and country-specific payroll configurations.
