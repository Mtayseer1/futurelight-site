Odoo for Pharmacies

A complete ERP, POS, and inventory automation solution designed for pharmacies in Jordan & the GCC.

Pharmacies operate in a fast-paced, precision-driven environment where mistakes are costly and efficiency is critical. Odoo provides pharmacies with an all-in-one ecosystem that automates inventory, manages prescriptions, tracks expiry dates, improves sales operations, and integrates seamlessly with accounting, HR, and purchasing.

Why Pharmacies Need Odoo

Unlike generic POS systems, Odoo offers pharmacy-specific features that help reduce losses, prevent stockouts, and maintain compliance. With integrated modules for sales, inventory, purchasing, and finance, pharmacies can operate smoothly with real-time accuracy.

  • Track medicine expiry dates and receive automatic alerts
  • Prescription handling and customer profiles
  • Fast POS system optimized for pharmacy workflows
  • Real-time inventory management with minimum/maximum levels
  • Full accounting integration for invoicing, payments & taxes
  • Multi-branch pharmacy support under one database
