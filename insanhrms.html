About the Partnership

Future Lights Technologies is an official technology partner of Insan HRMS, a leading cloud-based Human Resource Management System designed to simplify and automate HR processes. Together, we deliver digital transformation for HR departments across the Middle East — ensuring seamless integration, regional compliance, and scalability for modern enterprises.

About Insan HRMS

Insan HRMS provides a full suite of HR solutions — from payroll and attendance to performance and task management. It’s built for flexibility, offering secure cloud hosting, multilingual support, and integration-ready APIs.

  • Employee Self-Service (ESS) & Manager Self-Service (MSS)
  • Payroll, Compensation & Benefits Management
  • Personnel & Time Attendance Tracking
  • Performance & Task Management
  • Cloud and Mobile Accessibility
  • Compliance with regional HR laws & regulations
