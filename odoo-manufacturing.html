About Odoo Manufacturing (MRP)

The Odoo Manufacturing module (MRP) provides a powerful system for planning, scheduling, and controlling production operations. It allows businesses to define Bills of Materials, track work orders, manage quality checks, and integrate seamlessly with Inventory and Purchase modules.

At Future Lights Technologies, we implement Odoo MRP solutions tailored for manufacturers in Jordan and the GCC, ensuring optimized workflows, minimal downtime, and real-time reporting.

Key Odoo Manufacturing Features

Bill of Materials (BOM)

Define complex product structures and manage multi-level BOMs with real-time material consumption tracking.

Work Orders & Routing

Manage work centers, define routing steps, and monitor production efficiency through live dashboards.

Production Planning

Plan manufacturing operations based on sales forecasts, material availability, and demand planning algorithms.

Fully Integrated Manufacturing Ecosystem

Inventory

Real-time stock updates for raw materials and finished goods linked directly to production orders.

Purchase

Auto-generate purchase orders for raw materials when levels fall below thresholds.

Quality

Integrate with Odoo Quality module to manage inspections and quality control during manufacturing.

Maintenance

Track machine maintenance schedules and link preventive actions with work orders.
