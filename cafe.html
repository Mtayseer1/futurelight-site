Some of the Key Features of Our Café Management System

A complete end-to-end solution for cafés, combining customer experience with operational efficiency.

QR Code Ordering

Customers scan a unique table QR code to view menus, place orders, call waiters, and check their bill instantly.

Table Management

Real-time table statuses: Available, Reserved, Busy. Automates resets after payment collection.

Order Management

Add, edit, or void items dynamically. Keeps all orders and totals synchronized with tables.

Menu Management

Manage items with categories, prices, availability, descriptions, and images for customer clarity.

Kitchen Dashboard

Live order feed with preparation notes. Kitchen staff can mark items as completed in real-time.

Reporting & Analytics

Track KPIs, sales trends, top items, and table usage with rich visual charts and statistics.

Cash & Shift Management

Open/close shifts with starting and ending cash. Highlights discrepancies and secures accountability.

Waiter Mobile System

Waiters receive notifications, open tables, and add items — all on their mobile devices.

Payment Management

Supports multiple payment methods with receipt printing and table reset after settlement.

Purchases, Expenses & Unpaid Items

Tracks raw materials, overheads, and unpaid/complimentary items for transparent auditing.
