Café Management System

A complete digital solution for cafés and restaurants

Overview

Our Café Management System streamlines daily operations with digital efficiency. From QR code ordering and table reservations to waiter apps and kitchen dashboards, it’s designed to simplify café and restaurant management while delivering exceptional customer experiences.

Key Features

Table Management

Track table status in real time with reservation and availability updates.

QR Code Ordering

Customers can scan and order directly from their tables with zero wait time.

Waiter Notifications

Waiters receive live alerts when customers need service, place orders, or request bills.

Kitchen Dashboard

Kitchen staff can view new orders instantly and mark them as completed.

Billing & Receipts

Integrated billing with multiple payment methods and receipt printing.

Reports & Analytics

Powerful dashboards for sales, items, staff performance, and financial insights.
